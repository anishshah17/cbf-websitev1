Participation FAQ + Rules

Why should I participate?

First off - it's incredibly fun. You'll meet new friends and interact with experienced mentors along the way. Creating and running a business young helps kids gain hands-on skills for any career path.

How do I apply?

Applications must be submitted online. Submit one application for each business. Children working as a group should submit one application that includes each child’s information. No more than 3 participants per business.

What happens after I apply?

Applications are reviewed on a first come, first serve basis. We will get back to you within 5 business days.

Rules + expectations

This event is designed to give children the experience of selling a product or service. If a parent is found selling or promoting a child’s product or service, the child’s business will be disqualified.

Product guidelines

We will only be accepting businesses selling original, handmade items. Unfortunately, this means resellers will not be able to participate.
