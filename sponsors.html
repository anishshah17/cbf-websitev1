Sponsors

Help local kidpreneurs take the stage.

Sponsorship support helps power materials, mentoring, outreach, and a memorable fair-day experience for participating families.

Why Sponsor?

  • Reach families and community leaders across Naperville.
  • Invest directly in youth entrepreneurship and confidence-building.
  • Support a local event that celebrates student innovation.

Sponsorship Opportunities

Review sponsorship packages and choose the level that best fits your organization.

View Sponsorship Packages

Diamond Supporting Sponsorship - $1000

  • Fair is presented by your business.
  • Business name is included across promotional materials.
  • Featured keynote speaking opportunity at prefair workshops or fair.
  • Promotional table space at fair and prefair workshops.
  • Promotional materials included in participant goodie bags.
  • Recognition tied to awards at prefair workshops and fair.
  • Create a branded award.
  • Logo linked on website.
  • Direct access to waitlist and mailing list plus featured campaign placement.
  • Bimonthly Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok shoutouts during and after program.
  • Customized virtual workshop for kidpreneurs.
  • Access to professional photography for company marketing.
  • Opportunity to nominate two professionals as guest judges.

Gold Supporting Sponsor - $500

  • Promotional materials included in participant goodie bags.
  • Promotional table space at the fair.
  • Branded activity at prefair workshops.
  • Logo linked on website.
  • Access to professional photography for company marketing.
  • Recognition tied to specific awards at prefair workshops.
  • Monthly Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn shoutouts.
  • Promotional table space at prefair workshops.

Silver Supporting Sponsor - $250

  • Promotional materials included in participant goodie bags.
  • Recognition tied to specific awards at prefair workshops.
  • Logo linked on website.
  • Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn shoutouts.

Friend of the Fair Sponsor - $100

  • Name linked on website.
  • Community Supporter Certificate.
  • Early access to event recap and photos.
  • Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn shoutouts.
All proceeds are tax deductible and benefit the Naperville Children's Business Fair.
