About the Fair

Hosted by a committee of local Naperville high school students, the fair gives young entrepreneurs the chance to test the waters of business by selling their own products and services in a supportive environment.

Hands-on Entrepreneurship

Kids get real-world experience with planning, budgeting, marketing, and customer interaction. Past fairs have welcomed hundreds of kidpreneurs and thousands of community visitors.

Feedback + Awards

Participants receive feedback from high school judges and local business professionals. Awards recognize standout ideas and execution at the end of the fair.

Community Powered

Families, neighbors, and sponsors help make the fair a welcoming celebration of youth creativity and leadership.
